## General
- Add Audio (Mono) Track - Shift + A
- Add Instrument Track - Shift + I

## Track
- Select track: `up/down`
- Arm + monitor: `r`
- Monitor: `u`
- Solo: `s`
- Mute: `m`
- Delete/Remove track: `Shift + T`
